Alcoholic

California Lemonade

Ingridients

  • 2 oz Blended whiskey
  • Carbonated water
  • Juice of 1 Lemon
  • 1/4 tsp Grenadine
  • 1 tblsp Powdered sugar
  • Juice of 1 Lime

Directions

  • Shake all ingredients (except carbonated water) with ice and strain into a collins glass over shaved ice
  • Fill with carbonated water and stir
  • Decorate with slices of orange and lemon
  • Add the cherry and serve with a straw
